How do I open an account?

You just have to go to the home page and click on open an account and follow the instructions


How do I make a deposit?

You need a cash account (activated upon clicking on the link received by email during the activation process). You have several options to make a deposit: credit card, money order, paypal, bank debit, text message, and phone.


How do I cash out?

The same way as you have made a deposit, some fees may apply, please refer to the information section


Can I hold different position at the same time during a game?

Yes, you can hold as much position as you desire, remember you are competing to perform the best return.


Can I play on more than one table at the same time?

Yes, sure you can play by simply entering in a new competition and a new window will pop up


What happened if I get disconnected during a competition?

Your position will be hold and will not change during your absence, if the competition ends your positions will be closed and your return calculated
